Droidcon is a global developer conference series and a network focusing on the best of Android, and in 2018 it will be coming to Vietnam (again). The idea behind the droidcon conferences is to support the Android platform and create a global network for developers and companies. We offer high-class talks from different parts of the ecosystem, including core development, embedded solutions, augmented reality, business solutions and games. Meet a lot of rockstar international developers and level up your Android game.
